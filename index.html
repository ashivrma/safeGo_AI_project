// The Universal Help Signal

The 4-step
distress gesture

  • 1Hold your hand up facing the camera with fingers spread open
  • 2Tuck your thumb inward across your palm
  • 3Fold your four fingers down over the tucked thumb
  • 4Hold the closed fist for ~1.5 seconds facing the camera
